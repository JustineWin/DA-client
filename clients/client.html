Who We Are

For more than 50 years, Syracuse University Abroad (SU Abroad) has taken students across the globe to live, study, intern and connect in foreign communities. Every year, we send 2,000 students to over 60 countries. Our eight centers offer high-quality study abroad programs for students of all disciplines to study, intern, volunteer, and become a part of the local culture. Come join us for a semester, summer or academic year for an unforgettable experience. Start your journey at suabroad.syr.edu.

Diversity Statement

SU Abroad is committed to creating more – and more substantive and affordable – opportunities to study abroad for all students, especially those in STEM and health professions, veterans, and first-generation students, to produce productive and ethical global professionals.
